What is a Seed?

A seed is something you can provide. It might be your time, money, services or various other items like clothes.

What is a Need?

A need is anything you may need one time or on a recurring basis. A need can either be a service e.g a ride, a phone call, babysitting etc, money for bills or groceries, or items like clothing, toys, appliances, etc.
